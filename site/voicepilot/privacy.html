Privacy Policy — VoicePilot

Last updated: June 2, 2026

Thank you for using VoicePilot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

📱 1. Information We Collect

VoicePilot is a voice-assisted productivity app. Depending on features you use, we may process:

  • Account information — if you sign in (email, display name, or provider identifiers from Google or similar).
  • Voice and text input — audio you record or text you type to run commands, dictation, or AI-assisted actions.
  • App content you create — notes, tasks, shortcuts, or automations stored in the app or your account.
  • Device and usage data — app version, language, crash logs, and feature usage to keep the service reliable.
  • Purchase data — subscription status via Google Play (we do not receive full payment card numbers).

🎯 2. How We Use Your Information

  • Transcribe speech and execute the features you request.
  • Sync your data across devices when you are signed in.
  • Process subscriptions and restore purchases.
  • Improve performance, fix bugs, and develop new features.
  • Respond to support requests.

We do not sell your personal information.

🔔 3. Permissions

The app may request:

  • RECORD_AUDIO / microphone — for voice commands and dictation.
  • INTERNET — for sync, AI processing, billing, and updates.
  • Notifications — optional reminders or status alerts.

You can revoke permissions in your device settings; some features may stop working without them.

🎙️ 4. Voice, Audio, and AI Processing

When you use voice or AI features:

  • Audio or text may be sent to secure servers or third-party AI providers to generate a response.
  • We request processing only for the task you initiate, not for unrelated profiling.
  • Retention periods depend on the feature (e.g. local cache vs. cloud history); check in-app settings where available.
Do not use VoicePilot to process highly sensitive data (passwords, financial credentials, medical records) unless you accept the risks of voice and cloud processing.

🛡️ 5. Data Security

  • Data in transit is protected with industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel.
  • No security method is perfect; report concerns to us promptly.

🌍 6. International Transfers

Our service providers may process data in countries other than yours. We require appropriate safeguards where required by law.

👶 7. Children

VoicePilot is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children without parental consent.

📊 8. Analytics

We may use analytics and crash reporting to understand usage and stability. These tools are configured to minimize unnecessary personal data.

💰 9. Subscriptions and Ads

Paid plans are billed through Google Play. Google handles payment data under its own privacy policy.

VoicePilot does not sell your data to advertisers. If advertising is introduced later, this policy will be updated.

🔗 10. Third-Party Services

We may use providers such as Google Play, Firebase, authentication providers, and AI APIs. Each processes data under its own terms, only as needed to run VoicePilot.

✅ 11. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of account data by contacting us. You can also delete the app and cancel subscriptions in Google Play.

We may update this policy. The latest version is always at this URL.
